# Structure of Acidity / 酸味の構造

## Core Concept
Acidity gives coffee liveliness and dimension, distinguishing high-quality beans from commodity ones.
酸味はコーヒーに生き生きとした表情と立体感を与え、高品質な豆を汎用品から区別する要素である。

## Objective Mechanism (Facts)
- **Organic Acids**:
  - Citric (Lemon-like)
  - Malic (Apple-like)
  - Tartaric (Grape-like)
  - Acetic (Vinegar-like - usually defect if high)
- **Altitude**: Higher cultivation generally concentrates acids.

## Subjective Application (Perception)
- Positive: Bright, crisp, juicy, sparkling.
- Negative: Sour, sharp, biting, vinegary.

## Quotable Insight
> "Acidity is the spark that ignites the flavor profile."
> 「酸味とは、風味の輪郭に火を灯す火花のようなものである。」
