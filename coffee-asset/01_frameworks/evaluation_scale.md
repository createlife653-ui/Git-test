# Evaluation Scale / 評価スケール

## SCA Style (0-10)
- **6.0-6.9**: Good (Commodity/Commercial)
- **7.0-7.9**: Very Good (Premium)
- **8.0-8.9**: Excellent (Specialty)
- **9.0+**: Outstanding (Super Premium)

## Personal Enjoyment Scale (Subjective)
- **1**: Would not drink again.
- **3**: Daily drinker.
- **5**: Memorable experience.

## Quote
> "Scores are for communication; descriptions are for memory."
