# Roast vs Bitterness / 焙煎と苦味

## Gradient
1. **Light Roast**: Almost no bitterness. Tea-like.
2. **Medium Roast**: Balanced. Bitterness supports structure.
3. **Dark Roast**: Bitterness dominates. Carbon notes.

## Insight
Bitterness from roast (carbonization) is different from bitterness from extraction (tannins/caffeine).
- Roast Bitterness = Dry, smoky.
- Extraction Bitterness = Astringent, medicinal.
