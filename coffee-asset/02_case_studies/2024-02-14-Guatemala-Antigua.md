# 20240214 Guatemala Antigua / グアテマラ・アンティグア

## 1. Basic Data (Objective Information) / 豆の基本データ
- **Origin (産地)**: Guatemala, Antigua, Finca Filadelfia
- **Variety (品種)**: Bourbon
- **Process (精製)**: Washed
- **Roast Date (焙煎日)**: 2024/02/01
- **Roast Level (焙煎度)**: Medium-Dark (Full City)

## 2. Brewing Recipe / 淹れ方のレシピ
- **Method (器具)**: Hario V60
- **Ratio (比率)**: 20g : 300ml (1:15)
- **Grind Size (挽き目)**: Medium (Comandante 24 clicks)
- **Water Temp (湯温)**: 88°C
- **Time (抽出時間)**: 2:45

## 3. Sensory Log / 味と香りの記録
- **Aroma (香り)**: Dark chocolate, roasted almond, slight smoke.
- **Flavor (風味)**: Cacao, brown spice, orange zest.
- **Acidity (酸味)**: Muffled but present. Round.
- **Sweetness (甘み)**: Molasses-like.
- **Body (コク)**: Creamy and heavy.
- **Aftertaste (余韻)**: Long, clean, slight bitterness.

## 4. Analysis & Connection / 分析と考察
- **Observation (気づいたこと)**: The body was excellent, but the orange note was faint.
- **Hypothesis (仮説)**: The temperature (88°C) might have been too low to fully solubilize the brighter acids/esters, emphasizing the roast notes instead.
- **Reference (参照)**: See [Temperature Effect](../00_principles/temperature_effect.md)

## 5. Next Step
- [ ] Try 92°C to bring out more fruit notes.

## 6. Quotable Summary / まとめの一言
> "A classic Antigua that prioritizes comfort over complexity; a chocolate bomb with a whisper of citrus."
