# 20240214 Guatemala Antigua / グアテマラ・アンティグア

## 1. Basic Data (Objective Information)
- **Origin**: Guatemala, Antigua, Finca Filadelfia
- **Variety**: Bourbon
- **Process**: Washed
- **Roast Date**: 2024/02/01
- **Roast Level**: Medium-Dark (Full City)

## 2. Brewing Recipe
- **Method**: Hario V60
- **Ratio**: 20g : 300ml (1:15)
- **Grind Size**: Medium (Comandante 24 clicks)
- **Water Temp**: 88°C
- **Time**: 2:45

## 3. Sensory Log
- **Aroma**: Dark chocolate, roasted almond, slight smoke.
- **Flavor**: Cacao, brown spice, orange zest.
- **Acidity**: Muffled but present. Round.
- **Sweetness**: Molasses-like.
- **Body**: Creamy and heavy.
- **Aftertaste**: Long, clean, slight bitterness.

## 4. Analysis & Connection
- **Observation**: The body was excellent, but the orange note was faint.
- **Hypothesis**: The temperature (88°C) might have been too low to fully solubilize the brighter acids/esters, emphasizing the roast notes instead.
- **Reference**: See [Temperature Effect](../00_principles/temperature_effect.md)

## 5. Next Step
- [ ] Try 92°C to bring out more fruit notes.
