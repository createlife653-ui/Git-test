# v03 Image Prompts

## Common Style

Vertical 9:16 editorial illustration, cozy specialty coffee shop, warm wood, clear composition, no text, no logo, no watermark.

## Scene 1

Rows of coffee bean bags on a wooden shelf in a small roastery, labels intentionally blank, beginner standing in front thoughtfully, warm light.

## Scene 2

Four simple blank cards floating around a coffee bean bag, representing country, region, grade, process, clean educational composition, no written words.

## Scene 3

Coffee bean bag treated like a profile card or passport on a table, beans scattered, magnifying glass, warm editorial mood, no readable text.

## Negative Prompt

Text, logo, watermark, readable labels, brand names, distorted hands, extra fingers.
