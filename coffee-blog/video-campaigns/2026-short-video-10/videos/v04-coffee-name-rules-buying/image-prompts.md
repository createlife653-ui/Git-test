# v04 Image Prompts

## Common Style

Vertical 9:16 editorial illustration, specialty coffee roastery, warm light, soft shadows, no text, no logo, no watermark.

## Scene 1

Beginner choosing coffee beans in a small roastery, shelves of blank coffee bags, calm thoughtful atmosphere, warm wood interior.

## Scene 2

Three blank feature cards around a coffee bean bag, representing origin, farm, variety, elegant educational graphic style without text.

## Scene 3

Friendly roastery counter scene, customer asking a barista about beans, coffee bags with blank labels, warm and approachable.

## Negative Prompt

Text, logo, watermark, readable labels, brand names, distorted hands, extra fingers.
