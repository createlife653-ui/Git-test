# v02 Image Prompts

## Common Style

Vertical 9:16 editorial illustration, warm natural light, calm coffee learning mood, no text, no logo, no watermark.

## Scene 1

Close-up editorial illustration of a person tasting coffee without showing full face, warm steam rising from cup, thoughtful mood, soft background.

## Scene 2

Abstract aroma lines moving from cup to nose and throat, simple visual metaphor for retronasal aroma, coffee brown and cream tones, elegant educational style.

## Scene 3

Desk with tasting glasses, notebook, coffee cup, small arrows suggesting flavor spreading through mouth and aroma, no readable text.

## Negative Prompt

Text, logo, watermark, distorted face, extra fingers, brand packaging.
