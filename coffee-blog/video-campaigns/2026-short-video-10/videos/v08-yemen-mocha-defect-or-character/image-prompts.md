# v08 Image Prompts

## Common Style

Vertical 9:16 editorial illustration, quiet reflective coffee journal mood, dry warm texture, no text, no logo, no watermark.

## Scene 1

Old notebook, coffee cup, and scattered coffee beans on a wooden desk, soft evening light, reflective mood, no readable writing.

## Scene 2

Abstract balance scale with coffee beans on one side and dry aromatic spices on the other, symbolizing defect or character, elegant warm tones.

## Scene 3

Coffee cup with dry warm wind-like texture and lingering aroma lines, memory-like atmosphere, subtle paper grain.

## Negative Prompt

Text, logo, watermark, readable handwriting, distorted hands, brand packaging.
