# v01 Image Prompts

## Common Style

Vertical 9:16 editorial illustration, warm morning light, specialty coffee atmosphere, clean tabletop, subtle paper texture, no text, no logo, no watermark.

## Scene 1

Japanese home coffee table with a ceramic coffee cup, three small white dishes, sugar, salt, and vinegar bottle shapes without labels, calm educational mood, top-down composition.

## Scene 2

Abstract illustration of a tongue being tuned like a musical instrument with a tuning fork, warm coffee brown and soft cream palette, thoughtful and simple, no medical realism.

## Scene 3

Notebook with tasting marks, a cup of black coffee, small glasses of clear liquid, cozy desk lighting, reflective beginner learning mood, no readable text.

## Negative Prompt

Text, logo, watermark, distorted hands, extra fingers, readable labels, brand packaging.
