# v07 Image Prompts

## Common Style

Vertical 9:16 editorial illustration, historical coffee mood, warm desert light, deep wine and coffee colors, no text, no logo, no watermark.

## Scene 1

Dark coffee cup beside grapes and brown sugar, warm low light, elegant tasting note atmosphere, cinematic close-up.

## Scene 2

Yemen mountain coffee landscape as respectful illustrative scene, terraced hills, warm dry air, coffee plants, no people close-up, no text.

## Scene 3

Coffee beans, spices, old notebook, and a steaming cup on a wooden table, complex aromatic mood, deep warm palette.

## Negative Prompt

Text, logo, watermark, stereotypes, readable labels, brand packaging, distorted objects.
