# v05 Image Prompts

## Common Style

Vertical 9:16 editorial illustration, specialty coffee and fruit acidity, warm natural light, refined educational mood, no text, no logo, no watermark.

## Scene 1

Black coffee cup with a subtle glowing outline, citrus and apple shapes nearby, bright clean morning light, elegant composition.

## Scene 2

Abstract positive acidity as sparkling light around coffee and fruit, juicy and crisp mood, warm cream background, no text.

## Scene 3

Abstract contrast between pleasant round acidity and sharp unpleasant sourness, one side soft glowing curves, other side thin sharp lines, coffee cup centered.

## Negative Prompt

Text, logo, watermark, scary medical imagery, distorted fruit, brand packaging.
